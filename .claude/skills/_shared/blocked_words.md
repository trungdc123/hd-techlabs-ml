# Blocked Words & Phrases

## BLOCKED English Words (25 - instant reject)

robust, comprehensive, leverage, streamline, utilize, facilitate, enhance, optimal, seamless, cutting-edge, holistic, paradigm, innovative, transformative, pivotal, delve, commendable, noteworthy, meticulous, intricate, crucial, essential, fundamental, harness, underscore

## Tier 1 English (18 - instant red flags)

delve, landscape (metaphorical), tapestry, paradigm shift, leverage (verb), harness, navigate (metaphorical), realm, embark on a journey, myriad, plethora, multifaceted, groundbreaking, revolutionize, synergy, ecosystem (non-technical), resonate, streamline

## Tier 2 English (17 - suspicious in clusters of 3+)

robust, seamless, cutting-edge, innovative, comprehensive, pivotal, nuanced, compelling, transformative, bolster, underscore, evolving, fostering, imperative, intricate, overarching, unprecedented

## BLOCKED Vietnamese (14)

vuot xa, toan dien, tuyet doi, toi uu, trong yeu, noi bat, dac sac, xuat sac, an tuong, vuot troi, quan trong hang dau, dang chu y, dang ghi nhan, bat buoc

## BLOCKED Transitions

Moreover, Furthermore, Additionally, In conclusion, Consequently, Nevertheless, Nonetheless, Conversely, Subsequently, Hence, That said, That being said, With that in mind, Moving forward, When it comes to

Better: skip the transition, or use "because", "so", "but", "and". Let paragraph breaks do the work.

## BLOCKED Hedging

"It's worth noting", "It should be noted", "It's important to", "That being said", "With that in mind", "As such", "In today's...", "Serves as a", "Acts as a", "Functions as a", "It bears mentioning", "While there are certainly challenges", "This is not without its drawbacks", "To be sure", "To be fair", "could potentially possibly"

## BLOCKED Formatting

- Em dashes (-) - use hyphens (-) or commas
- Markdown (**bold**, `code`, ## headers) in evaluation text
- Curly quotes ("...") - use straight quotes ("...")
- Perfectly symmetric pros/cons
- Template-like repetitive phrasing
- Emoji-decorated headings
- Title Case In Every Heading
